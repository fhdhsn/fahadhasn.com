01 — Why ChargePK

EV ownership in Pakistan shouldn't mean range anxiety. Stations are scattered with no single reliable map — planning a Lahore-to-Islamabad trip means asking around in Facebook groups. ChargePK fixes that.

02 — Features

Everything an EV driver needs

Built end to end for Pakistan's roads — from motorway fast chargers to mall parking AC points.

🗺️

Interactive Charger Map

Every station in Pakistan on a live map — color-coded by AC or DC, with price per kWh and your distance to each. Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, the motorways, and the northern areas.

🛣️

Journey Planner

Enter your start and destination — by search, current location, or dropping a pin — and see every charger within 25 km of your route, plus total distance and drive time.

🧮

Charging Calculator

Set your charging amperage, battery capacity, and current charge level — get your exact charging time and, if you set when you plug in, the exact time you'll be done.

📋

Full Station Details

Connector types (CCS2, CHAdeMO, Type 2), charging power, prices, amenities, and contact numbers — so you know exactly what's waiting before you arrive.

🧭

One-Tap Navigation

Found your charger? Tap Navigate and you're in turn-by-turn directions instantly. Tap Call to confirm availability before you drive.

🔒

Private by Design

No account, no tracking, no analytics. Your location never leaves your phone — it's processed entirely on-device, only to show you nearby chargers.

03 — How it works

Three steps to a stress-free trip

01

Open the map

See chargers near you instantly, sorted by distance, price, or rating. Filter to DC fast chargers when you're in a hurry.

02

Plan your route

Heading out of town? The journey planner maps every charging option along the way, so you always know where your next top-up is.

03

Charge with confidence

Check the price and connectors, calculate your charging time, navigate there, and get back on the road.
